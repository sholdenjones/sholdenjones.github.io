We performed a common garden experiment using cleistogamous and chasmogamous seeds of 
<i> D. californica</i> sourced from eight populations along a latitudinal gradient. 
Cleistogamous seeds outperformed chasmogamous seeds despite being smaller, and there 
being no evidence of local adaptation.
